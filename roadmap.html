Roadmap

Thalem is just getting started. Below is our vision for how we plan to develop and expand our products over time. Our roadmap is ambitious but grounded in reality – we prioritise quality and sustainability over hype.

Near‑Term Goals

  • Launch Thalem Armwrestling on Google Play: release the app publicly once private testing is complete and core features are stable.
  • Polish Core Features: refine logging tools, improve performance and fix bugs based on tester feedback.
  • Add Languages: introduce Greek, Russian and Hindi so more athletes can use the app in their native language.

Mid‑Term Goals

  • Cosmetic Shop: launch a store for themes and cosmetic upgrades that let users personalise their app while supporting development.
  • Enhanced Analytics: add advanced charts, training insights and export features for coaches and athletes who want deeper analysis.
  • Web Version: create a web‑based version of Thalem Armwrestling at armwrestling.thalem.space for those who prefer to use a larger screen.

Vision

  • AI Coach: explore AI‑driven coaching and personalised suggestions based on your training data (subscription optional in the future).
  • 3D‑Printed Equipment: integrate with a line of 3D‑printed handles and training tools designed specifically for armwrestlers.
  • More Strength‑Sport Tools: expand the Thalem platform to serve other strength sports while maintaining our focus on quality and affordability.
