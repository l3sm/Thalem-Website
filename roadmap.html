Roadmap

Thalem is still at the beginning, but I know where I want to take it. I’m building slowly and carefully, making sure things actually work in real life training before moving on. Quality first, no rushed hype.

Near‑Term Goals

  • Launch Thalem Armwrestling on Google Play: release the app publicly once private testing is done and the core features feel stable and solid.
  • Polish Core Features: keep refining logging tools, fixing bugs and improving performance based on what real users experience.
  • Add Languages: start adding more languages like Greek, Russian and Hindi so athletes from different countries can use the app comfortably.

Mid‑Term Goals

  • Cosmetic Shop: launch a theme and cosmetic store so people who enjoy the app can support development and customise the look.
  • Enhanced Analytics: add deeper charts, insights and export options for athletes and coaches who want more detailed analysis of their training.
  • Web Version: build a web-based version of Thalem Armwrestling at armwrestling.thalem.space for people who prefer a bigger screen.

Vision

  • AI Coach: experiment with AI-driven coaching and personalised suggestions based on your training data (this would likely be an optional subscription later).
  • 3D-Printed Equipment: connect the app with a line of 3D-printed handles and training tools designed specifically for armwrestlers.
  • More Strength-Sport Tools: expand Thalem to support other strength sports while keeping the same focus on quality and fair pricing.
