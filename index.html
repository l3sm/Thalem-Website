Thalem – Tools for Strength Athletes & Armwrestlers

I’m building Thalem because strength athletes deserve proper tools. If you love training and want something built by someone who actually trains too, you’re in the right place.

What is Thalem?

Thalem is my personal project. I want to help armwrestlers and other strength athletes track their training in a serious, structured way. Just proper logging and progress tracking.

My goal is to help grow strength sports by making training more organised and accessible, especially in a sport like armwrestling that doesn’t have many good tools yet.

Thalem Armwrestling App Features

Program Builder

Create and customise armwrestling specific training programmes with ease.

Workout & PR Logs

Track reps, weights and personal records over time to monitor progress.

Table Sparring Logs

Record sparring sessions including opponents, rounds and results.

Opponent Management

Keep a list of opponents, track matches and analyse how you stack up.

Stats & History

Visualise your progress with charts and detailed historical data.

Cosmetic Themes

Personalise your app with sleek themes from the in-app shop.
