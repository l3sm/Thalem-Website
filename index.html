Built by athletes, for athletes. Thalem is a small independent company creating digital tools to help strength sport enthusiasts track, analyse and improve their training.

What is Thalem?

Thalem is a mission-driven software company focused on building technology for strength athletes. Our first product is Thalem Armwrestling – an app designed by a competitive armwrestler that makes serious training accessible and affordable.

Our goal is to grow niche strength sports like armwrestling by lowering the barrier to structured training. By using data and discipline rather than gimmicks, Thalem aims to provide athletes with tools they can trust.

Thalem Armwrestling App Features

Program Builder

Create and customise armwrestling-specific training programmes with ease.

Workout & PR Logs

Track reps, weights and personal records over time to monitor your progress.

Table Sparring Logs

Record sparring sessions including opponents, rounds and outcomes.

Opponent Management

Keep a list of opponents, track matches and analyse how you stack up.

Stats & History

Visualise your progress with charts and detailed historical data.

Cosmetic Themes

Personalise your app with sleek themes from our in‑app shop.
