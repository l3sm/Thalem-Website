Privacy Policy

This page explains how I handle data while Thalem is in private testing. I’m one person building this project and I want to keep things as simple and transparent as possible.

1. Who I am

I'm Aggelos, the creator of Thalem and the Thalem Armwrestling app. I train armwrestling, bodybuilding and powerlifting, and I’m building tools for strength athletes.

2. What this policy covers

This policy applies to:

  • the Thalem website (thalem.space and its subpages), and
  • the private test builds of the Thalem Armwrestling app.

3. No data collection during testing

During the current testing phase, I do not collect, store or process any personal data from the app.

  • No accounts
  • No cloud sync
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No remote database

All training data in the test builds stays only on your device (local storage). If you uninstall the app or clear your browser/app data, that information is lost and I do not have a copy.

4. Contact & early access form

The contact form on this website is currently a simple front-end form. It does not send your data to a backend server or database.

If you choose to email me directly at my personal email pollatosangelos@gmail.com, your email and any personal information you include are handled by your email provider and mine (for example Gmail). I will only use your email to:

  • reply to you, and
  • organise early access testing if you asked for it.

I don’t sell or share these emails with anyone.

5. Cookies & trackers

I’m not using Google Analytics, tracking pixels or any other third-party analytics tools during this testing phase.

6. Future changes (public release)

When Thalem Armwrestling is released publicly and if I add things like crash reports, optional accounts or analytics, I will update this privacy policy and clearly explain:

  • what data is collected,
  • why it’s collected, and
  • how you can opt out where possible.

Any future features that involve personal data will follow EU privacy rules (GDPR) as I’m based in Europe.

7. Your rights & contact

If you have any questions about privacy, or you want me to delete emails you’ve sent me, you can contact me at:

pollatosangelos@gmail.com
