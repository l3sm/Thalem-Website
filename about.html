The Story of Thalem

Thalem started from something simple: I couldn’t find a serious, accessible way to track armwrestling and strength training the way I wanted. So instead of waiting for someone else to build it, I decided to make it myself.

I built this using modern tools, lots of experimenting, and honestly way too many late nights. Thalem is my attempt to give strength athletes something that actually understands how we train.

About Me

I'm Aggelos. I’m 18 and I train armwrestling, bodybuilding and powerlifting. When I’m not on the table or under a barbell, I’m studying computer science and building Thalem piece by piece.

My goal is simple: help armwrestlers and strength athletes get stronger by giving them tools that actually support real training instead of fitness fluff.

My Philosophy

I believe progress comes from data and consistent work. I don’t add pointless features or fake marketing nonsense. If something doesn’t help you improve, I don’t add it.

I also want Thalem to be affordable. The full version is just €3 because I want anyone to be able to use it. Getting stronger shouldn’t require paying a subscription every month.
