The Story of Thalem

Thalem was founded by Aggelos, a passionate armwrestler, bodybuilder and powerlifter. As a competitor in strength sports, he recognised the lack of serious and accessible tools for logging training sessions. Instead of waiting for someone else to build them, he decided to do it himself.

Built using a combination of modern programming and AI tools and countless hours of dedication, Thalem represents the belief that athletes deserve software as obsessive as they are about their craft.

About the Founder

Aggelos is an 18‑year‑old strength athlete and computer science student from Greece. When he's not on the table armwrestling or lifting heavy weights, he's writing code and studying how to turn his vision for Thalem into reality. His goal is to help the sport grow by giving athletes a powerful training logger at an affordable price.

Our Philosophy

At Thalem we believe that data and discipline lead to progress. We refuse to fill our products with fluff or gimmicks. Instead, we focus on giving athletes the tools they need to track, analyse and improve – nothing more, nothing less.

Because Thalem is built by athletes, we understand how training fits into real lives. Our pricing is designed to be fair and accessible – just €3 for the full version of our first app – so that no one is priced out of excellence.
