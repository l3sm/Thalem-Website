Thalem Armwrestling

Thalem Armwrestling is my main project: a complete training logger built specifically for the sport. I designed it around how I actually train and what I couldn’t find in other apps.

Instead of a generic gym tracker, this app is focused on real table time, strength work and long-term progress for armwrestlers.

Detailed Features

Every feature here comes from real training experience. I didn’t build this as a generic fitness app – it’s for armwrestlers who want structure and progress.

Custom Programme Builder

Craft your own armwrestling training plans using an intuitive programme builder I built for armwrestlers. Define exercises, sets and progression to suit your goals.

Workout Tracking

Log every rep, weight and set of your strength training. Easily track PRs and adjust as you progress.

Table Sparring & Opponents

Record sparring sessions, score your rounds and maintain a history of matches against opponents.

Stats & Analytics

View charts and summaries of your volume, intensity and win/loss ratios to identify trends over time.

Personalisation

Unlock cosmetic themes from the in-app shop to give your app a look that matches your style – or keep it classic.

Pricing & Availability

I want Thalem Armwrestling to stay accessible. When it launches on Google Play, you’ll be able to choose what works best for you:

  • Free (ads): Same core logging tools, supported by ads (planned).
  • Full (€3 one-time): All features with no ads. Pay once, use it forever.
  • Cosmetics: Optional themes in the shop, around €0.50 each or bundles of about €5, if you want to support the project and customise the look.
