Thalem Armwrestling

Our flagship product is a comprehensive armwrestling training logger. Designed by a competitive athlete, it brings serious tracking to a sport that has long been underserved by mainstream fitness apps.

Thalem Armwrestling lets you build customised programmes, record workouts, track table sparring sessions, manage opponents and view detailed statistics. It’s built to be both powerful and easy to use so you can focus on getting stronger.

Detailed Features

Custom Programme Builder

Craft your own armwrestling training plans using our intuitive programme builder. Define exercises, sets and progression to suit your goals.

Workout Tracking

Log every rep, weight and set of your strength training. Easily track PRs and adjust as you progress.

Table Sparring & Opponents

Record sparring sessions, score your rounds and maintain a history of matches against opponents.

Stats & Analytics

View charts and summaries of your volume, intensity and win/loss ratios to identify trends over time.

Personalisation

Unlock cosmetic themes from our shop to give your app a look that matches your style – or keep it classic.
